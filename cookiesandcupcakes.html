Cookies and Cupcakes

Ingredients of the cookies

  • 1 Banana
  • 50 gr. oatmeal
  • Handful of nuts, almonds, chocolate and coco

Ingredients of the cupcakes

  • 1 apple
  • 50 gr. oatmeal
  • 20 gr. cocoa powder
  • 1 honey tablespoon

Preparation

For the cookies: Put in a bowl banana and crush it very well, later put oatmeal and Handful of nuts, almonds and chocolate, mix all very well and take a part of the mix and give it a form cookies. Put them in a can and to oven to 200 cº for 25 minutes.

For the cupcakes:Put all ingredients in the processor until that it is well mixed, later put mix in a mold and to oven to 200cº for 20 minutes.

Apple Cake

Ingredients

  • 250 gr. cut apples
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 gr. oatmeal
  • 1 tablespoon vinager
  • 2 tablespoon yeast
  • 30 gr. coconut oil
  • 100 gr. coconut sugar, panela or xylitol
  • cinnamon

Preparation

Place the coconut sugar with the eggs in a bowl and beat very well, then add the vinegar, coconut oil and continue beating until well integrated. Now mix the flour, the yeast and the cinnamon, this mixture is added little by little to the previous mixture and beat well so that no lumps remain. Cut the apples in squares so added to mixture and mix well. Grease the mold so that not the cake sticks, add mixture to mold y finally put in oven preheated a 175 c for 45 minutes. Let cool to unmold and that's it.

Banana Flan

Ingredients

  • 6 bananas
  • 6 eggs
  • 600 ml milk
  • 2 spoonful whole sugar

Preparation

First prepare the candy, in a pan put the whole sugar and move constantly until that is melted, put in a mold and spread for all sides. Put in a blender the bananas, the eggs and the milk and liquefy until that is very integrated. Later put in a mold the mixed and to oven for 1 hour to 200 cÂ°.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 280 ml. Skim milk
  • 1 tbsp Apple cider vinegar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 54 gr. Applesauce
  • Stevia to taste
  • 2 tbsp Coconut oil
  • 120 gr. Oatmeal
  • A pinch sea salt
  • 2 gr. Yeast
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • 140 gr. Carrot
  • Almond to taste
  • Nut to taste

Preparation

First grease a mold, after in a bowl put 280 ml. skim milk, apple cider vinegar and mix. In other bowl put 2 eggs, applesauce, stevia to taste, coconut oil, the mixed of the milk and mix very well. After put 120 gr. oatmeal, cinnamon, sea salt, 2 gr. yeast, mix and put 140 gr. carrot, almond, nuez and again mix until that well integrated and go to greased mold and bake to 180 cยบ by 40 minutes let cool for 10 minutes and ready for serve.

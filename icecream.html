Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 150 gr. Greek yoghurt
  • 10 gr. Melted chocolate
  • 1 Morocho cake
  • 1 crushed banana

Preparation

Put in a bowl the crushed banana, greek yoghurt and mix very well. Later melt the chocolate and cut the morocho cake in pedacitos very small. Finally put the mix in a glass and on top put the morocho cake in pedacitos and mix and put the melt chocolate and to freezer compartment for 4 hours.

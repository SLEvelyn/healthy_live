Yogurt Cake

Ingredients

  • 100 gr. Greek Yogurt
  • 100 gr. Requeson
  • 1 egg
  • Stevia to taste
  • 20 gr. Oatmeal
  • Blue Berries to taste
  • Peach to taste

Preparation

Put in a bowl the egg, yogurt, requeson and stevia to taste and mix very well. Next put the oatmeal in the mixed and mix very well, for the decoration put blueberries and peach to taste , next put mix in a mold for microwave and introduce to microwave by three minutes and ready.

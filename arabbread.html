Arab Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1 spoonful yeast
  • 1 spoonful oil olive extra virgen
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

Put in a glass water the yeast and mix very well. In a bowl put the flour, the spoonful oil, salt to taste and the mixed of yeast with water and mix very well later use your hands so knead until is well compact. put the dough in a bowl covered in the oven for 2 hours.
Take the dough and dividing into portions of 45 gr and strech with a roll until give a round shape, repeat this process with the other portions, finally put the dough in a pan very hot and when some bubbles appear it is time to turn the bread Repeat this process with the other portions and clever.

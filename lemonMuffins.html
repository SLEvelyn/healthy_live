Lemon Muffins

Ingredients

  • 30 gr. Oatmeal
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • Stevia to taste
  • 10 gr. Chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp Yeast
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Virgin Olive Oil
  • Lemon zets
  • Two pieces of chocolate equivalent to 8 grams, that is, each piece 4 grams.

Preparation

First put in a bowl, the egg, the tbsp of virgin olive oil, 10 gr. chia seeds and mix very well. In other bowl put 30 gr. oatmeal, stevia to taste, the tbsp of yeast, cinnamon to taste and mix well. Put the mix of the dry ingredients to the recent mixed and mix very well, after go to the greased molds, put in every mold a piece of chocolate and to bake to 200 cยบ by 20 minutes or until the toothpick comes out clean and ready for server.
